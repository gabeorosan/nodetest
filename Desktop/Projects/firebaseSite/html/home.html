Treatment Philosophy

I specialize in the treatment of eating and mood disorders. Anorexia, Bulimia, and Binge Eating, as well as Depression, Anxiety, and Bipolar disorder form the bulk of my practice. These problems take significant time end effort to treat; successful therapy requires a commitment to regular weekly appointments.

Desirable Difficulty

Therapy is a process of self-reflection and challenging your preconceptions. This is a difficult practice, and can be painful at times. It is my job to guide your therapy so that you develop a better understanding of yourself and become more resilient as a result, along with helping you progress towards your personal goals.

About Me

I am a licensed clinical psychologist specializing in mood and eating disorders, and I have a private practice in Ann Arbor, Michigan. I have practiced for over 25 years as a therapist, and worked as an intern and fellow at Harvard Medical School and Beth Israel Hospital.
